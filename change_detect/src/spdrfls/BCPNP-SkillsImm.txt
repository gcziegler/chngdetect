BC PNP - Skills Immigration
The Skills Immigration (SI) stream of the BC Provincial Nominee Program (BC PNP) is for people with the skills, experience and qualifications needed by B.C. employers. If you would like to settle in B.C., the SI stream can help you become a permanent resident.
Topics in this section
Skilled Worker
See if your skills and occupation meet the labour needs of B.C.’s growing economy.
Healthcare Professional
Get help finding a health care job in B.C., and apply for permanent residence.
International Graduate
Recent graduate from an eligible Canadian institution? Continue your career in B.C.
International Post-Graduate
Put your B.C. natural, applied or health sciences post-graduate degree to work in B.C.
Entry Level and Semi-Skilled
See if you qualify in a valid tourism/hospitality, long-haul trucking, food processing or other occupation.
Skills Immigration process
Guidance around the steps to gaining permanent residence through the Skills Immigration stream.

Get answers to common questions about the Skills Immigration stream.
