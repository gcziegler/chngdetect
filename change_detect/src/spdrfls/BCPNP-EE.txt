BC PNP - Express Entry BC
Do you have the skills to fill a high-demand occupation in B.C.? The Express Entry BC (EEBC) stream of the BC Provincial Nominee Program (BC PNP) connects to the federal Express Entry system. It’s a faster way for eligible skilled workers to gain permanent residence in B.C. 
Topics in this section
EEBC — Skilled Worker
See if your skills and job meet the labour needs of B.C.’s growing economy.
EEBC — Healthcare Professional
Get help finding a health care job in B.C., and apply for permanent residence via Express Entry BC.
EEBC — International Graduate
Recent graduate from an eligible Canadian institution? Continue your career in B.C.
EEBC — International Post-Graduate
Put your B.C. natural, applied or health sciences post-graduate degree to work in B.C.
